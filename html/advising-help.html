Applying Credits to your Credential
Students registered in multiple programs (any combination of Honours/Major/Minor) should check to make sure that units for upper division courses are assigned to one program or the other, but not both.
Units applied to one certificate may also be applied to Honours/Major/Minor degree programs, but may not be applied to another SFU certificate or diploma.
